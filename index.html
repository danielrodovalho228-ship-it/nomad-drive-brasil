FAQ

Good questions, straight answers

Can I rent without a referral?

Right now we work referral-first to keep things trusted and safe. If you don't have one, reach out anyway — tell us your situation and we'll see what's possible.

Can I drive with my foreign license?

Generally a valid foreign license is accepted in Brazil for visitors, often alongside an international permit. We confirm the exact requirement with you before pick-up.

What happens if the car has a problem?

You get a backup car so you're never stranded, and we stay reachable for your whole trip.

Is there a deposit?

Yes — a refundable security deposit is part of the contract. The amount is confirmed in your quote.

Which cities do you serve?

Pick-up is in Uberlândia, MG. From there the car is yours to travel anywhere in Brazil within the contract terms.

How do I pay?

We'll arrange a convenient method when you book — discussed openly, with everything in writing.
